About Me

GIS Analyst & Remote Sensing Specialist

I'm a Geography and GIS graduate from the University of Ghana with hands-on experience in spatial analysis, land cover mapping, field data collection, and environmental monitoring. My work sits at the intersection of satellite technology and real-world conservation challenges.
